Questions
Frequently Asked
How do I send footage?
After purchase, we send you a link with exact instructions — what angle to film from, how far away, how many sprints we need. Phone camera is fine. We'll guide you through everything so we get what we need for a proper analysis.
What equipment do I need?
A phone with a camera and somewhere to sprint. That's it. Film from a side angle, about 15-20 metres away. Slow motion mode helps but isn't essential — we can work with standard 60fps footage.
How long until I get my analysis?
Your full analysis and report will be delivered within 5-7 business days of receiving your footage. For The Program and The Full System tiers, your 12-week program is delivered alongside the analysis.
Does this actually work remotely?
Virginia Champion — State Champion, 10.54s 100m, 10 D1 scholarship offers. Full remote client from the USA. She never set foot in Sydney. The system works because the analysis is based on measurable biomechanical data, not eyeballing. The data doesn't care where you are.
What sports does this work for?
Any sport where speed matters. Football, rugby, athletics, cricket, basketball, AFL. The biomechanical principles of efficient sprinting are universal. We've worked with athletes across 10+ sports.
What if I'm not happy with the analysis?
In 200+ analyses, we've never had an athlete say the analysis wasn't valuable. You'll see things about your mechanics that no one has ever shown you before. If you're serious about getting faster, the data speaks for itself.
Can I upgrade from The Analysis to The Program later?
Yes. If you start with The Analysis and want to upgrade, we'll credit your $499 toward The Program or The Full System. No double-paying.
